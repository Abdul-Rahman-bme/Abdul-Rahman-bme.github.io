Projects

Analog Automatic Solar Tracker (RaDAR)

University of Moratuwa | Sep 2025 – Dec 2025

Fully analog single-axis solar tracker designed to maximise photovoltaic energy capture without microcontrollers. Utilised LDR-based differential sensing, analog PID control with op-amps, PWM motor control via L298N, and DC motor actuation. Designed a custom 4-layer PCB in Altium Designer, simulated in LTspice, and fabricated with a SOLIDWORKS-designed enclosure.

Temperature-Controlled Vaccine Cold Chain Monitoring System

University of Moratuwa | Oct 2025 – Nov 2025

Low-cost cold-chain monitoring solution for vaccine transportation in low-resource settings. Focused on sensor integration, wireless alerts, regulatory pathway analysis (FDA Class I/II), and functional validation using the Biodesign methodology.

Smart Posture Cushion (P.A.P.A.Y.A.) – PulseCode

University of Moratuwa | Feb 2025 – Jul 2025

Smart cushion providing real-time posture feedback using FSR sensors, ultrasonic sensing, ATmega328P processing, and vibration alerts. Included custom PCB design, SolidWorks enclosure modelling, battery management, and user-centred design principles.
